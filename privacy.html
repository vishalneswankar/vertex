RewardX Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

RewardX is a rewards app where you play games and earn coins. You sign in only with your Google account. We do not offer username/password registration.

Information we collect

  • Google account info: email, name, and profile photo (when you sign in)
  • App data: coin balance, game activity, referral code, Bitcoin withdrawal address (if you add one)
  • Device data: device identifiers used for ads and analytics
  • Crash and usage data: via Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics

How we use data

  • Operate your account and reward balance
  • Process withdrawal requests
  • Show ads (Unity Ads)
  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Send optional push notifications

Third-party services

  • Google Sign-In and Firebase (authentication, database, analytics, crash reporting)
  • Unity Ads (advertising)

Data security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

See our account deletion page for steps to request deletion of your account and data.
