Delete Your RewardX Account

RewardX lets you sign in with your Google account (OAuth). If you want to delete your RewardX account and the data we store, follow the steps below.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to vertexappsteam@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Delete my RewardX account
  3. Send the email from the same Google email address you use to sign in to RewardX
  4. We will confirm and delete your account within 30 days
Note: Signing out of the app does not delete your account or data. You must email us to request deletion.

Data that will be deleted

  • Google account link (email, display name, profile photo)
  • Coin balance and game progress
  • Bitcoin withdrawal address
  • Referral code and referral history
  • Push notification token
  • Profile and app activity stored in our database

Data we may keep

  • Withdrawal/payout records (kept up to 7 years for fraud prevention and legal compliance)
  • Anonymous, non-identifiable analytics data

After deletion, your account cannot be recovered and any remaining coin balance will be lost.
